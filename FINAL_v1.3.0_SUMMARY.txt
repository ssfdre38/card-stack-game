   - Default: Enabled
   - Must draw if no valid plays

4. 🚀 Jump-In Rule (Advanced)
   - Default: Disabled
   - Play identical card out of turn
   - Chaotic gameplay!

5. 7️⃣0️⃣ Seven-Zero Rule (Fun Variant)
   - Default: Disabled
   - 7 = swap hands
   - 0 = rotate hands
   - Dramatic strategy changes!

6. 📈 Progressive Draw (Card Stacking)
   - Default: Disabled
   - +2 on +2 = 4 cards
   - Massive card draws possible!

7. 🎯 Force Play
   - Default: Disabled
   - Must play if you have valid card
   - No strategic drawing

8. 🎲 Challenge Wild +4 (Advanced)
   - Default: Disabled
   - Challenge illegal Wild +4
   - Bluffing element
